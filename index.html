Addition , Subtract, Multiplication and Division of Integers & Fractions :

Integers as Fractions :

Integers can be written as fractions by placing them over the denominator 1.
Example 1: 5 = 51, -3 = -31
This helps in performing operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division of integers with fractions.

Addition , Subtract, Multiplication and Division of Integers & Fractions :

Addition/Subtraction:

  1. Write the integer as a fraction (eg : 3 = 31)
  2. Find the LCD of denominators.
  3. Add/Subtract the numerators and keep the denominator.

Example: Addition
3 + 25 = 31 + 25 = 3 × 51 × 5 + 25 = 15 + 25 = 175

Multiplication:

Multiply numerators and denominators directly.
Example: 2 × 35 = 21 × 35 = 2 × 31 × 5 = 65

Division:

Flip the second fraction and multiply.
Example: 5 ÷ 23 = 51 ÷ 23 = 51 × 32 = 5 × 31 × 2 = 152

Evaluate Numerical Expressions Involving Fractions :

A numerical expression with fractions includes numbers, fractions, and operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. To simplify these, we follow a step-by-step process, often guided by the BODMAS rule (Brackets, Orders, Division/Multiplication, Addition/Subtraction).
Example : Simplify : 23 + 56 × 34

Step1 : Solve multiplication first:
56 × 34 = 5 × 36 × 4 = 1524 = 58

Step2 : Add 23 + 58

LCM of 3 and 8, which is 24.

23 + 58 = 2 × 83 × 8 + 5 × 38 × 3 = 1624 + 1524 = 16 + 1524 = 3124

Tips for Simplifying

  1. Always reduce fractions to their lowest form when possible.
  2. Be careful with signs (positive and negative fractions).
  3. Follow the BODMAS/PEMDAS rule step by step to avoid errors.
